About Us

Introduction
Asad Furnishing has become one of the leading players in up in coming Oman. Having an Omani background in the field of Furnishing Goods, we promise to bring elegance into your home from ours.Our mission statement reflects our proud past and our desire for a better future. We strive to provide the best products at the best prices, along with an exceptional service. Being the best and bringing the best into your home is our relentless pursuit. Asad Furnishing began with mere 2 employees in 1999, with a small office in the heart of the city (Al-Khuwair). Today our company is 40 employees strong, with local showrooms, and a warehouse with readily available stock. The centralized location of the business enables us to provide superior services to our valued customers.
